Completing Requirements

Keep your placement on track by reviewing every requirement, uploading proof, and finishing assigned eLearning modules directly inside your CPNW dashboard.

Accessing the Requirements list

  1. From the dashboard, open the My Records panel on the left and click Requirements.
  2. Filter the list by All, Outstanding, or Completed to focus on what needs attention.
  3. Each entry displays the requirement name, current status, and expiration date (if the requirement is due annually). When scrolling left to right, it also shows the type of requirement, frequency (annual or once), the category, and who reviewed (approved, rejected, etc..) the requirement.

Use the column headers to sort by Requirement Name, Status, Expiration, Type, Frequency, or Category. Scroll horizontally to view every column and action.

Opening requirement details

  • Click the requirement name (first column) to open details. eLearning modules launch in a new browser tab so you can complete the training.
  • All other requirements open a modal with instructions, linked documents to download, submission or declination fields (when applicable), an Upload Documentation link, and a Save button.
  • Use the message board on the right side of the requirements view to communicate with your program coordinator, facility coordinator, or CPNW Reviewer (where applicable).

Uploading documentation

  • Use the Upload button within each requirement to attach immunization records, or other required documentation.
  • Supported formats include PDF, JPG, and PNG, Excel Spreadsheets (.xlsx), and Word Documents (.docx).
  • After submission, the item shows as Pending Review until a coordinator approves it. You will see comments if additional information is needed.

Completing eLearning modules

eLearning modules appear in the Requirements list and will open in a new browser tab when clicked.

  1. Launch each module, review all sections, and pass the quiz. You must score 80 or better to pass. You may take the quiz as many times as necessary to pass.
  2. Once finished, the module automatically records the completion. Your status, score and expiration date will be updated to the requirements table. You may have to refresh your browser window to see the updated information.

Pro Tip: Read the instructions for each requirement carefully before submitting. Most rejections happen because directions were missed or not followed. If a requirement asks for dates/locations, or has any other fillable field, they must be utilized when possible. For immunization requirements it is not enough to just upload the documentation and click Save. You must also enter the requested information into the required fields.
