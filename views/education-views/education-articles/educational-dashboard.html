Educational Coordinator Guide

Coordinator Dashboard

Get familiar with every section of the landing page so you never miss a student message, compliance alert, or action item.

Notifications (Right Side)

Every time you land on the Dashboard, the right column keeps you current. This widget lists new student messages, reminders about pending approvals, and platform announcements. Click a notification to jump to the relevant area or open the message thread.

Requirements Quick Link

The first shortcut in the left column opens the Requirements builder. Educational Coordinators do not have compliance items to complete themselves; instead, this area is where you create new or edit existing educational requirements for your programs. Update instructions, attach documents, and publish so students see the latest guidance.

My Account

Expanding My Account reveals three links:

  • My Profile – Confirm your contact information, campus, and role.
  • My Security Settings – Change your password, two-factor delivery method, or preferred verification channel.
  • My Demographics – Review what your healthcare partners see when they view your coordinator record.

Admin Menu

Open the Admin section on the left to access the tools you use most:

  • Review – Takes you to the requirement review queue where you can approve, reject, or conditionally approve student uploads.
  • Assignments – Assign cohorts to specific clinical facilities and confirm rotation details before they publish.
  • Reports – Generate compliance summaries, view background check and OIG/SAM status, and open each student’s Docs folder for supporting files.
  • Users – Manage active accounts across your programs: approve new student/faculty requests, archive or reactivate records, and monitor access.
  • Background + WATCH Reports – Available when your program uses Checkr and/or CPNW WATCH, this link launches background checks and tracks their status.

Tips for Staying Current

  • Start each day by scanning Notifications so no student messages or compliance reminders are missed.
  • Use the Requirements link to review expiring items before sites flag them.
  • Keep the Users tab tidy—archive graduates quickly and reactivate returning faculty as needed.
