Healthcare Coordinator FAQs

Answers for facility coordinators overseeing rotations, site requirements, and user access.

Search filters the accordion and suggests deep-dive guides for complex workflows.

The Reports view only displays records six weeks before a scheduled start date. Students drop off automatically two weeks after their end date. They also must be marked Verification Complete by their education coordinator or CPNW DVS team before you can see them. If a student still doesn’t appear, confirm their assignment dates and verification status.

Those columns summarize requirement completion. A “+” icon means every requirement in that category (CPNW Clinical Passport, Education, or Healthcare) is complete. A “–” icon signals that at least one requirement still needs attention. Click the icon to open the status popup and review each requirement line by line.

Healthcare coordinators have view-only access to the CPNW Clinical Passport list. You can open documents, read reviewer comments, and reference approved records, but only education coordinators or CPNW DVS staff can change those statuses. You can approve, reject, or waive your own site-specific requirements from the same modal.

Open Requirements from your Dashboard and click Create Requirement. Complete the form field by field: name, facility (Required By), category (Site Orientation or Forms), frequency, discipline, user type, abbreviation, status, due date, and requirement instructions. Site Orientations can be auto-approved, while Forms can offer waiver options. Attach any reference files and use the link icon to point students to external resources.

The Active Users tab only contains healthcare coordinators tied to your facility. Account Requests lists new healthcare staff who completed registration—open each request to approve or decline it. Deactivated accounts live in the Inactive Users tab, so you can reactivate them later without recreating access.
